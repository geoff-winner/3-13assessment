Assessment 2 specs

1. Inputting a single letter and outputting a count of 1 for that single letter.

2. Inputting two letters of the same letters and outputting a count for those 2 letters.

3. Inputting two different letters and getting a count for those two letters.

4. Inputting three of the same letters and getting a count of those.

5. Inputting a combination of three different letters and getting a count for those.

6. Inputting words and getting a count for those.
